## 2025-03-12 - AppNavBar and SyncIndicator Toggle ARIA\n**Learning:** When using `ButtonVariant::Icon` for toggles (search, sync settings), screen readers lack context of the button's expanded state and its controlled panel.\n**Action:** Add `"aria-expanded": "{state()}"` and `"aria-controls": "panel-id"` to toggle buttons, and match the target container with `id: "panel-id"`. This is specifically needed for non-native toggles without built-in accessibility semantics.
